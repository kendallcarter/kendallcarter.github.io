4x4 Animal Style

Kendall, Sam, Manny, and Will are 4x4 Animal Style. 4x4 Animal Style is an emerging band from the south suburbs of Chicago. Their style can be primarily classified as indie, rock or alternative, but they also take inspiration from blues, rock, jazz, R&B and more. They've performed all over Chicago and the Chicagoland area, as well as in the emerging Illinois college scene, at schools such as Illinois State University, University of Illinois Champaign Urbana, University of Illinois Chicago, and more. In the summer of 2022 they released their debut single, "Raft", and in the following winter released their eponymous debut EP, "4x4 Animal Style". They're currently working on a new album, to be released in 2023.
